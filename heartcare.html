OUR PRODUCT

IMPULSE-12 ECG Monitors for home use can help you track your heart rhythm and rate. If you have certain heart condition, including artial Fibrillation, it may be important to keep track of your heart rhythm. You Might also want to monitor your heart rate and heart rhythm for other reasons, like when you are exercising.

WHEN TO USE IMPULSE-12

Reconfiguration of emergency services could lead to patients with life-threatening conditions travelling longer distances to hospital. Concerns have been raised that this could increase the risk of death. We aimed to determine whether distance to hospital was associated with mortality in patients with life threatening emergencies.

Once a man called a doctor because his father was having chest pain. After doctor arrived, he suggested to move patient to the nearest cardiac hospital for ECG test. This process took really long time, before reaching to hospital, the patient got into critical condition. This condition could have been prevented by portable "IMPULSE-12" ECG machine. IMPULSE-12 could be used by doctor on the spot, and man would not be dealing with this critical condition.

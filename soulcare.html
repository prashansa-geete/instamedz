CAREER

The future is already uncertain, and picking one thing to do can feel overwhelming. For some, this creates career anxiety. With increasingly myriad career options rising, career counseling has become an essential part of people's lives. Career counseling in India is gaining momentum, With the increase in awareness of the importance of career counseling amongst the parent population, there has been more and more parents voluntarily enrolling their children for career counseling. The Government also lays emphasis in providing for compulsory career counseling and guidance to the students at school level in order to help them chart a proper career path and choose therewith.

STRESS MANAGEMENT

When depression saps your confidence and motivation, it’s tempting to isolate or do the bare minimum. As a result, normal life tasks such as work and relationships start to fall by the wayside. Eventually, doing the bare minimum creates new problems, which cause stress, anxiety and worsening depression. The cycle continues, stronger than before. Depression doesn’t just affect one person—it also affects those around them. Oftentimes, friends and family feel lost. They want to help, but they don’t know where to begin. So, here we are, to help you avoid this unthinkable fate.

YOGA AND MEDITATION

Meditation and Yoga. It is rightly said, ‘that you only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough.’ Meditating to release your inner toxins is the best way to purify your mind. In the same manner, performing yoga helps you get rid of extra negativity that secretly resides in your body. Your yoga practice becomes your fire escape from daily turmoil. It is a tool to diminish anger as your mental peace is important. Discover yourself through meditation and yoga.We provide you such relaxing benefits and tips to keep your anxiety away from you.

ANGER CONTROL

Anger is a basic human emotion triggered by an emotional hurt, a feeling that occurs when one is mistreated, is griefing or is feeling an obstacle in the path of attaining personal goals. Memories of traumatic or enraging events can also make one angry. Anger management doesn’t involve getting rid of anger but using anger to enhance your life. Our goal is to reduce both your emotional feelings and the physiological arousal that anger causes. Because anger is one letter away from DANGER.

MUSIC HEAL AND PREACHINGS

Music is a uniquely effective tool for treating neurological impairment because it recruits every region which spurs activity and fosters connections across a wide track of brain regions typically involved in emotion, reward, cognition, sensation and movement. When music is performed intensively for months, melodic intonation therapy produces long- term gains that appear to arise from changes in neural circuitry- the creation of alternative pathways in the brain.
